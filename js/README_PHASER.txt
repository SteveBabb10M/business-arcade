IMPORTANT: Phaser is not included by default.

Download Phaser 3.55.2 from the official CDN and save it as:

    js/phaser.min.js

Recommended source:
https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/phaser@3.55.2/dist/phaser.min.js

In your browser:
1. Open the link above.
2. Right-click the page -> "Save As..."
3. Save as phaser.min.js into this folder (js/).
